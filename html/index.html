Introduction

DAMS is a program designed to read, parse, and analyze data using various data structures such as graphs. It has been developed as part of the Desenho de Algoritmos (Algorithm Design) course at FEUP (Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto) on the Bachelor's of Informatics and Computing Engineering degree, made by Diogo Vieira, Tiago Pinto, and Antero Morgado.

The primary goal of DAMS is to process data efficiently and provide valuable insights through data analysis.
